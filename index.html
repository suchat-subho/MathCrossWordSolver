How to Play Math Crossword

Solve math clues and fill the grid just like a crossword puzzle.

Rules

  • Each clue is a math problem.
  • Answers must fit the grid length.
  • Only numbers allowed.
  • Intersecting cells must match.

Controls

  • Select a Token (Green boxes) at the bottom.
  • Drag and Drop the selected number to the blank cell in the
  • Press Enter for next clue.

Tips

  • Start with easy clues.
  • Use crossings to verify.
  • Double-check calculations.

Getting Started

  • To start a sample game, open any Sample Gi.
  • Copy the TSV content (Game Matrix) from the sample page.
  • Paste the "Game Matrix" into the textbox in the left pane.
  • Click "Load Grid" to begin the game.
